Our Academic Approach

At Marwadi University, we offer a diverse range of academic programs designed to meet the evolving needs of industry and society. Our curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications, ensuring students are well-prepared for their professional careers.

Faculty of Engineering & Technology

Computer Science & Engineering

Duration: 4 years (B.Tech)

Specializations: AI & ML, Data Science, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing

Learn cutting-edge technologies including programming, algorithms, software engineering, and emerging fields.

Mechanical Engineering

Duration: 4 years (B.Tech)

Specializations: Automotive, Robotics, Manufacturing

Master the principles of mechanics, thermodynamics, materials science, and design.

Civil Engineering

Duration: 4 years (B.Tech)

Specializations: Structural, Environmental, Construction Management

Learn to design, construct, and maintain infrastructure projects including buildings, roads, and bridges.

Electronics & Communication

Duration: 4 years (B.Tech)

Specializations: VLSI, Embedded Systems, IoT

Study electronic circuits, communication systems, signal processing, and embedded technologies.

Electrical Engineering

Duration: 4 years (B.Tech)

Specializations: Power Systems, Renewable Energy, Control Systems

Focus on electrical systems, power generation, distribution, and renewable energy technologies.

Information Technology

Duration: 4 years (B.Tech)

Specializations: Web Technologies, Mobile Computing, Network Security

Master IT infrastructure, web development, database management, and network administration.
