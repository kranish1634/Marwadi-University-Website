Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our admissions page or visit the campus during office hours. Fill out the application form and submit required documents.

What are the campus visiting hours?

The campus is open for visitors from Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Sundays and public holidays are closed.

How can I get scholarship information?

Contact our admissions office or visit the admissions page for detailed information about available scholarships and eligibility criteria.

Is campus tour available?

Yes, campus tours are available. Please schedule an appointment by calling our admissions office at least 2 days in advance.

How do I contact a specific department?

You can find department-specific contact information in the table above or contact our main office, and they will direct you to the appropriate department.

What documents are required for admission?

Required documents include mark sheets, transfer certificate, entrance test scores, ID proof, and photographs. See our admissions page for complete list.
