Admission Process

Step 1: Online Application

Fill out the online application form with your personal and academic details. Upload required documents.

Step 2: Entrance Test

Appear for the university entrance examination or submit valid entrance test scores (JEE, GUJCET, etc.).

Step 3: Counseling

Attend counseling session for document verification and course selection based on merit.

Step 4: Admission Confirmation

Pay admission fees and confirm your seat. Receive admission letter and student ID.
